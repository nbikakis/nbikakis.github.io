_about

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electronic Engineering at the Hellenic Mediterranean University and a Research Associate at the Information Management Systems Institute of the ATHENA Research Center, Greece. Before that, I was a research engineer in industry, a postdoctoral researcher at the Athens University of Economics & Business, the University of Ioannina, and the ATHENA Research Center.
I received my PhD in Computer Science in 2016 from the National Technical University of Athens, under the supervision of Prof. Timos Sellis. In 2009 I received my Diploma in Electronic & Computer Engineering from the Technical University of Crete.
